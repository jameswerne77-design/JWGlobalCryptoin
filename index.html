Write-Up

About JW Global Crypto

We started JW Global Crypto with a simple complaint about the industry: most platforms ask you to trust a dashboard instead of trusting math.

Digital assets were supposed to remove the need for blind trust — that's the entire premise of a public ledger. Somewhere along the way, the industry rebuilt the old banking model on top of it: deposit your coins with us, trust our balance sheet, hope the audit is real.

JW Global Crypto is our attempt to do it differently from day one. We are a digital-asset advisory and portfolio-intelligence platform, not a custodian. When you connect a wallet here, you're not depositing anything — you're simply letting the page read what's already publicly visible on the blockchain and present it back to you clearly, in one place, without a browser extension full of jargon.

We're a young firm. JW Global Crypto is currently a forming entity, working through the registrations and licensing that a serious advisory practice requires before it can responsibly manage client capital. We'd rather tell you that plainly than dress up a logo and a landing page as something we're not yet.

What we offer today is portfolio visibility and advisory groundwork: a clean, secure way to view your own holdings, and a team building toward licensed advisory services as those approvals come through. What we don't offer — yet, and not without a license — is custody, managed funds, or guaranteed returns. Anyone who tells you otherwise about a platform like ours isn't being straight with you, and neither would we be.

Security Architecture

The best security chain is the one that asks for nothing

Here's exactly how the connection works, in plain terms — not marketing language.

01

Non-custodial by design

Your private keys never leave your own wallet. We never see them, store them, or have a recovery path to them — because there is no path to build.

02

Read-only connection scope

The connection requests permission to view your public address and balances only. It does not request transaction-signing permissions for transfers.

03

Open wallet standard

We connect through WalletConnect, an open, widely audited protocol used across the industry — not a proprietary connector that only we control.

04

Disconnect anytime, from either side

You can revoke the connection from your wallet app at any moment with zero downstream effect on your funds, because we never had a claim on them.

05

No password vault to breach

There's no account password tied to your funds for an attacker to phish, because your funds were never tied to an account on our servers.

06

Verify everything yourself

Every balance shown here can be independently checked on a public block explorer. We'd rather you double-check us than take our word for it.
